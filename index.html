Curriculum Alignment

Level 1 — Early (P1, Ages 5-6)
Short words, counting to 20, simple addition/subtraction, basic reading
Level 2 — First (P2-P4, Ages 6-8)
Longer words, place value, multiplication introduction, paragraph comprehension
Level 3 — Second (P5-P7, Ages 8-10)
Complex spelling, all four operations, fractions, rich comprehension texts

Aligned to Scottish Curriculum for Excellence — Literacy & English and Numeracy & Mathematics.

About Classmates

Version: 1.1

Content: 34 learning modes, 18 stories, 230+ spelling words, 31 achievements

Subjects: Literacy, Numeracy, Geography, Challenges

Curriculum: Scottish Curriculum for Excellence (Early, First, Second Level)

Features: Adaptive difficulty, sound effects, per-pupil tracking, avatar builder, certificates, worksheets, whole-class mode, custom quiz builder, class leaderboard

Data: All data stored locally on this device. No internet required.
